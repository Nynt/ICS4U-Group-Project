Home of the humans and the only planet in the Solar System to inhabit life. Earth’s biodiversity has expanded significantly since its early life, dotted with several mass extinctions along the way. With the formation of the ozone layer in Earth’s atmosphere, the majority of the Sun’s harmful radiation was blocked, allowing life to flourish on the planet. The human race are dependent on Earth’s limited resources, and are constantly changing the natural lifecycle of the planet.
